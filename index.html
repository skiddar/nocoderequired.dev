NO CODE REQUIRED

The Non-Technical Manager's Guide to Engineering Teams

by Scott Darden

You didn't study technology. Now you're managing engineers — and every book on engineering leadership assumes you can already read code. This one doesn't.

Get the book What's inside

No Code Required is written specifically for non-technical leaders running technical teams. Drawing on thirty years in engineering and six stints as CTO, Scott Darden delivers a practical, no-nonsense guide to earning your engineering team's respect, making better technical decisions — without pretending to be technical — and building a team that actually ships.

What's inside

Part I

Seeing Magic

Understand what your team actually does — its culture, composition, process, and output — without needing to read a line of code.

Part II

Channeling Magic

Earn trust and respect, direct the work, decide when to outsource, and set expectations that match reality.

Part III

Amplifying Magic

Make better, faster decisions, hire great people, keep them motivated, and become a force multiplier for your team.
